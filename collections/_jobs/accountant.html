Open North is seeking an Accountant who has high attention to detail and a strong sense of accountability.

Why join Open North?

Join a growing and amazing team of people who are passionate about helping improve democracy. Come make a big contribution to increasing citizen participation, government transparency and accountability!

Open North is seeking an Accountant who has high attention to detail. You will help maintain accurate financial records for the organization under the supervision of the Director of Operations.

The successful candidate has demonstrable professional accounting and bookkeeping experience, unwavering ethics, and high personal accountability for a job well done. Also important is a positive, problem-solving attitude and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Strong listening ability, teamwork, and communication skills are highly desirable traits.

Details

  • Location: Montréal
  • Type of position: Full-time permanent
  • Salary: $50,000 - $55,000
  • Benefits: Free coffee, bright sunny office in the Mile End, flexible schedule.
  • Start date: Immediately

Benefits after 3 months

  • Comprehensive Health Benefits
  • Group RRSP plan
  • Cell phone allowance

To Apply

  • Please attach a single PDF file that includes a cover letter and your resume and send to jobs@opennorth.ca
  • We look at cover letters closely, therefore applications without cover letters will not be considered

Key responsibilities

Reporting to the Director of Operations the successful candidate will:

  • Record accounting transactions in QUICKBOOKS
  • Report on expenses monthly, quarterly and/or annually according to contract requirements (project-based)
  • Record supplier invoices (project-based)
  • Prepare and enter employee expense reports (project-based)
  • Issue of A/R invoices (project-based)
  • Manage A/P
  • Prepare and process quarterly GST and QST reports
  • Create Journal Entries and perform Bank Reconciliations
  • Payroll accounting (project-based)
  • Asset Management

Minimum Educational Requirement

  • Diplôme d'études collégiales (DEC), Accounting & Management Technology or Attestation of Collegial Studies (AEC) - Financial Management

Mandatory experience

  • Minimum 2-3 years of prior professional full-cycle bookkeeping experience in a project-based environment
  • Proficient in Quickbooks Desktop
  • Good working knowledge of GAAP

Required qualifications

  • Accuracy and attention to detail is absolutely required, as well as a track record of reliability, confidentiality, and conscientious work habits
  • Bilingual, written and spoken English and French
  • Strong knowledge of bookkeeping practices and procedures
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, specifically Excel, and Google Suite Apps (Docs, Sheets, Calendar, Drive)
  • Ability to work independently
  • Ability to adapt and work efficiently in a rapidly changing dynamic environment with deadline pressures

Desired qualifications

  • Previous experience working with a Quebec-based nonprofit and social enterprise.

Personality Traits

  • Reliable
  • Willingness to multi-task
  • Outcomes-oriented
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Desire to learn
  • High threshold for ambiguity
