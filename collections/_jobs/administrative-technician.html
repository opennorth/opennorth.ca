Open North

Open North is Canada’s leading not-for-profit social enterprise specializing in open data and the development of online tools that promote civic engagement, transparency and accountability. Open North works with governments, media and civil society to connect citizens and decision-makers and to provide citizens with the information and tools they need to track and influence the decision-making processes of government. Within a short period, Open North has established itself as a leader in open government and open data and online budget consultations.

Why Join Open North

Join a growing and amazing team of people who are passionate about helping improve democracy. Come make a big contribution to increase citizen participation, government transparency and accountability!
Open North needs a fluently bilingual Administrative Technician who is a natural multi-tasker, with superb organizational skills and proficient in working with with cloud-based file management systems.
The successful candidate is discreet, has superb customer relations experience and strong attention-to detail. Equally important is a positive, problem-solving attitude and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Strong listening ability, teamwork, and communication skills are highly desirable traits.
Join an awesome dynamic, flexible and fun work environment, with an opportunity to contribute to the growing transparency and online engagement world.

Key Responsibilities

Reporting to OpenNorth’s Executive Director the successful candidate will work with senior management to:

  • Act as the primary contact for questions and requests.
  • Coordinate communication-related activities, including website content updates and translation, in coordination with OpenNorth’s lead developer and communications staff.
    • Coordinate website development needs with web developer
  • Coordinate logistics across the organization:
    • Arrange meetings and travel and accommodation arrangements for the OpenNorth team
    • Organize travel receipts and prepare expense reports
    • Maintain inventory and coordinate office supplies and equipment needs, including office purchases such as laptops and software licenses
    • Organizational document management practices and archiving, including end of project and program evaluations
      • Maintain cloud-based file management systems (Google Drive and Dropbox) as well as physical filing systems
    • Coordinate internal documentation activities: documentation of organizational processes and development of training manuals on key aspects of the organization
      • Includes: updating policies, contracts, employee handbook
  • Prepare correspondence
  • Maintain a database, file access permissions, staff accounts, and mailing lists
