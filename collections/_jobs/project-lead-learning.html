OpenNorth is looking for one (1) Open Smart Cities Project Lead with advanced analysis and policy experience, learning program management and design, and project leadership.

Note: this is a mid-level position.

The Government of Canada announced that Evergreen, working in partnership with OpenNorth, their Future Cities Canada collaborative platform and numerous non-profit institutions, foundations and stakeholders across Canada, will receive funding to deliver activities from the new Smart Cities Community Support Program (SCCSP). The funding supports advisory and capacity-building services for communities nation-wide as they explore and implement smart cities approaches. OpenNorth is the SCCSP’s technical lead responsible for the one-to-one advisory services for cities and communities across Canada. SCCSP funding currently runs from October 2018 until March 2020.

OpenNorth

Founded in 2011, OpenNorth is Canada’s leading not-for-profit organization specialized in open data, open government, community engagement, open smart cities and civic technology. We work with Canada’s largest, most innovative, and connected cities. Our network extends across the country and globally through communities of practice and open government partners. Our work promotes the effective use of data as infrastructure facilitating sustained engagement between government, civil society, and the public.

Job description

As part of the OpenNorth team under the leadership of the Executive Director, and working in close coordination with Evergreen and program partners, the successful candidate will work under the supervision of OpenNorth’s SCP Program Program Director and the Executive Director. The SCP Program team currently includes a Program Director, Program Manager, Project Lead, and two Analysts, each with experience from a range of backgrounds. OpenNorth’s SCP Program team focuses on specific domain areas: data management, procurement, governance, citizen engagement, hardware and software. OpenNorth is hiring two Project Leads, one focused on Learning Program Management and the other on Technical aspects of smart cities. All candidates for SCP Program positions must demonstrate advanced knowledge in all SCP Program domain areas above. They will work closely with OpenNorth’s Applied Research Lab and coordinate the input of SCCSP Program partners and clients.

They will apply their learning knowledge and pedagogical experience to their understanding of civic and socioeconomic issues in Canadian communities, and critical data issues such as data literacy, digital divides, and social equity in the application of technologies. They will be able to synthesise information from multiple different bodies of knowledge and stakeholders, in order to plan and coordinate the implementation of a learning path and educational curriculum for Canadian communities with respect to the above-stated critical data issues.

Responsabilities

  • Implement and monitor a comprehensive learning path and coordinate curriculum content for Canadian communities, in key topics focussed on data and technology
  • Manage and guide junior staff in conducting and analyzing quantitative and qualitative data collected on Canadian communities to diagnose maturity levels and assess problems
  • Monitor progress of Canadian communities throughout the duration of the program according to the program KPIs and evaluation framework
  • Maintain service delivery standards to meet the capacity building needs of tailored capacity building action plans developed with city and community clients
  • Provide deep analysis on specific open smart city capacity building domains as they apply to individual cases
  • Manage the development of capacity building module content with domain expert advisors
  • Manage the implementation of the program’s learning path for one-to-one advisory services clients
  • Maintain the case management system - track and record interactions with communities - as part of the integrated program structure with Evergreen
  • Coordinate outcome-based reports for program milestone reports and market analysis purposes
  • Manage Junior Analysts, in coordination with the second Project Lead (Technical)
  • Reporting on program activities for OpenNorth. Actively contribute to impact focused program-wide reporting
