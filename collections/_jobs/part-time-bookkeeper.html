Open North is seeking a reliable part-time bookkeeper who has high attention to detail.

Why join Open North?

Join a growing and amazing team of people who are passionate about helping improve democracy. Come make a big contribution to increasing citizen participation, government transparency and accountability!

Open North is seeking a reliable part-time bookkeeper who has high attention to detail. You will help maintain accurate financial records for the organization under the supervision of the Executive Director and working closely with the Operations Manager.

The successful candidate has demonstrable professional bookkeeping experience, unwavering ethics, and high personal accountability for a job well done. Also important is a positive, problem-solving attitude and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Strong listening ability, teamwork, and communication skills are highly desirable traits.

Join a dynamic, flexible and fun work environment: free coffee, bright sunny office, surrounded by plants.

Details

  • Location: Montréal
  • Type of position: Part-time - 3 days per week, maternity leave 1-YR replacement
  • Salary: $20-25 an hour, leading to full-time for the right candidate.
  • Benefits: Free coffee, bright sunny office in the Mile End, flexible schedule.
  • Posting period: October 24-November 01, 2019
  • Interviews: November 07-12, 2019
  • Start date: November 18, 2019

To Apply

  • Please attach a single PDF file that includes a cover letter, your resume, in both French and English to jobs@opennorth.ca
  • We look at cover letters closely, therefore applications without cover letters will not be considered

Key responsibilities

Reporting directly to the Executive Director, the successful candidate will:

  • Work with Open North’s Operations Manager to perform full-cycle accounting procedures, maintain financial records and maintain a high standard of bookkeeping for the organization.
  • Archive financial documentation as necessary.
  • Prepare financial reports for monthly budget meetings with the Executive Director.

Minimum Educational Requirement

  • Diplôme d'études collégiales (DEC), Accounting & Management Technology or. Attestation of Collegial Studies (AEC) - Financial Management.

Mandatory experience

  • Minimum 2-3 years of prior professional full-cycle bookkeeping experience.
  • Proficient in Quickbooks Desktop.
  • Good working knowledge of GAAP.

Required qualifications

  • Accuracy and attention to detail is absolutely required, as well as a track record of reliability, confidentiality, and conscientious work habits
  • Bilingual, written and spoken English and French.
  • Strong knowledge of bookkeeping practices and procedures
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, specifically Excel, and Google Suite Apps (Docs, Sheets, Calendar, Drive).
  • Ability to work independently
  • Ability to adapt and work efficiently in a rapidly changing dynamic environment with deadline pressures.

Personality Traits

  • Reliable
  • Willingness to multi-task
  • Outcomes-oriented
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Desire to learn
  • High threshold for ambiguity

Desirable experience

  • Experience working with project-based financial structures.
  • Previous experience working with a Quebec-based nonprofit and social enterprise.
